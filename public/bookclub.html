A Neighborhood Experience

How it works

The neighborhood book club is all about connecting people, ideas, and passion. Each month, we meet at a member's house to discuss 'the book of the month'. We take turns acting as the member host. You are assigned a hosting date, and when it's your turn to host, you provide wine and dessert to snack and chat. Oh, and when you are the host for the month, YOU get to choose the book of the month to be discussed. Join us for stimulating conversation and great friendships.
